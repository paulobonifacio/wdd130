History

Adventure Consultants is the result of a group of friends' unwavering passion for adventure and nature. Each of them had a story of fascination with the rapids, the vertiginous descents, and the feeling of freedom that only rafting can offer. United by the desire to share this passion, they decided to create a company that not only offered rafting trips but transformative experiences.

They wanted every person who paddled with them to feel the same thrill and deep connection with nature that led them down this path. Thus, Rafting Company Adventure Consultants became more than a company; it became a family, dedicated to unforgettable adventures and the preservation of the wild rivers they love so much.
